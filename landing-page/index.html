Privacy-first by default

No tracking network required.

Reactions use a per-site dedupe hash instead of storing raw visitor IP addresses for the reaction flow. Email captures store explicit consent metadata, and retention controls keep raw reaction data on your terms.

Reaction storage
Custom WordPress tables with one current reaction per visitor and post.
Consent records
Captured emails include consent timestamp, source, reaction type, and consent text version.
Data controls
CSV export, retention cleanup, and explicit delete-on-uninstall behavior.
