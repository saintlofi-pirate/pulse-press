Reaction Set: Vertical Engagement

Alex Rivera

Product Designer @ Pulse

The future of digital publishing isn't just about reading; it's about the physical sensation of participation. This vertical engagement set is designed to anchor the reader's journey, providing a constant yet non-intrusive avenue for feedback.

By placing interaction tokens in the margin, we respect the content's integrity while acknowledging the social layer. The "slide-out" labels solve the icon-only ambiguity problem without cluttering the initial viewport. Each interaction triggers a tactile spring animation, giving the user immediate, rewarding sensory feedback.

Tactile Motion

Leverages cubic-bezier timing for that organic "pop" when hovering over interaction points.

Visual Weight

Counts are integrated into the flow to provide immediate social proof without breaking the layout.
